Overview

Purpose

In “White Water Rafting” we bring the best place for you and your family to have the greatest time. We look forward to give you all a fun time and adrenaline necessary for making your life happier, and strengthen relationships with those you come here. Today's world brings us a lot of information, work, and anxiety, which produces stress that affects negatively your life; we seek to eliminate it and renew yourself while you appreciate the nature. Located in the “Rio Grande Rexburg”; we are taking advantage of this amazing natural resource to attract tourist from all the country and even foreign ones, contributing this way to the growth of our amazing town.

Audience

Rexburg is a city full of students. These young people are smart and they do big efforts to become better people and good citizens. For an extremely low price, they can come and have a break from all the study activities that often let them down, and after they do that, they feel fresh and motivated to give their best in their studies. In Rexburg there are also families with children, who are looking for new amazing activities. They can also come and experience a high quality family time they will never forget.
